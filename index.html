About the Game

Up or Down is a simple number prediction game combined with a wallet system. Your goal is to guess whether the next number will go UP or DOWN.

A random number between 1 and 9 is generated each round.

  • You choose how much money to bet before each game.
  • Choose UP if you think the next number will be higher.
  • Choose DOWN if you think the next number will be lower.
  • Click Give Up to keep your current winnings.
  • If you survive 5 rounds, you win and get your reward back to your wallet!
  • If you guess wrong, you lose your bet.

Rewards increase based on difficulty and randomness. Manage your wallet wisely!
